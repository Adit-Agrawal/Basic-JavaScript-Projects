Commonly Asked Questions

Who is Harry Potter and what makes him special in the wizarding world?

Harry Potter is a young wizard known for surviving Lord Voldemort’s curse as a baby, which made him famous and marked him as the Chosen One who would eventually defeat the Dark Lord.

What is the significance of the Hogwarts houses and what do they represent?

Hogwarts houses—Gryffindor, Slytherin, Ravenclaw, and Hufflepuff—sort students based on traits like bravery, ambition, intelligence, and loyalty to help nurture their unique magical abilities.

What role does Dumbledore play in Harry’s life throughout the series?

Albus Dumbledore is the wise and powerful headmaster of Hogwarts who acts as a mentor, protector, and strategist guiding Harry through battles against Voldemort and his followers.

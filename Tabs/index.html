history

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry was founded over a thousand years ago by four powerful wizards: Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin. Disagreements, especially over student selection, led to Slytherin's departure. The school has since become the premier institution for magical education in Britain, surviving wars, dark wizards, and countless secrets.

vision

Hogwarts envisions a safe haven where young witches and wizards can learn, grow, and master their magical abilities. It fosters courage, wisdom, loyalty, and ambition through its four houses. Above all, it stands as a beacon of unity, tradition, and magical excellence.

goals

Hogwarts aims to provide a comprehensive magical education to young witches and wizards. It seeks to cultivate character, courage, wisdom, and loyalty through its house values. The school strives to prepare students to use magic responsibly and contribute to the wizarding world.
